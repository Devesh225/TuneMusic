COURSE STRUCTURE

Basic Acoustic:   ₹1000 / MONTH

An acoustic guitar course is a beginner-level program designed to teach individuals the basics of playing the acoustic guitar. It covers the fundamental techniques and skills necessary to play the instrument, including strumming, chord progressions, fingerpicking, and basic music theory.

View Course
Advanced Acoustic:   ₹1450 / MONTH

An advanced acoustic guitar course is designed for individuals who have already learned the basics of guitar playing and are looking to improve their skills and knowledge. This course typically covers advanced techniques such as fingerstyle playing, alternate tunings, chord progressions, and music theory.

View Course
Basic Electric:   ₹1000 / MONTH

A basic electric guitar course is an ideal starting point for individuals who are new to playing the guitar. The course focuses on introducing students to the basic principles of guitar playing, such as how to hold the instrument, how to tune it, and how to play chords and simple songs.

View Course
Advanced Electric:   ₹1450 / MONTH

An advanced electric guitar course is designed for players who have already acquired a basic understanding of the instrument and are looking to take their skills to the next level. This course covers advanced techniques such as sweep picking, alternate picking, legato playing, and tapping.
